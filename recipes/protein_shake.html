Protein Shake Recipe

Description

Enjoy a rejuvenating and refreshing protein shake after a hard workout!

Ingredients

  • Protein Powder
  • Water
  • (optional) Fruit

Steps

  1. Add 2 scoops of protein power into a blender
  2. Add 16 ounces of water into the blender
  3. If you want to have fruit blended it, add it in at this stage
  4. Blend your protein shake until you like the consistency
  5. Pour out onto a cup and enjoy
