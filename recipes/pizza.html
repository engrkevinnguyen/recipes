Pizza Recipe

Description

Enjoy your Friday night with a simple and lovely pan pizza topped with olives, cheese, greens, and tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Brocolli
  • Olives

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 425 degrees Fahrenheit and flatten out the dough.
  2. Spread tomato sauce throughout the pizza dough
  3. Add cheese over the tomato sauce
  4. Add your toppings over the cheese
  5. Place your pizza in your preheated oven for 20 minutes
  6. After 20 minutes, take out your pizza, slice it, and serve it with a drink of your choice!
